The Story.

In a quiet town in the countryside, there exist beings that are unpredictable and dangerous.

The locals call them evil spirits. The city folk call it all a hoax.

Others might think it an animal, human, or eldritch abomination.

But you know that whatever it is you're dealing with is something else.

Help Brennan find a means to survive, or let it have it's way with you...

A psychological horror game.


I am You is a short, psychological horror story with mystery elements.

Read newspaper clippings and listen to the ramblings of the townsfolk.

Gather important belongings left behind by former residents.

Sort the clues together,

and figure out the true nature of it.
